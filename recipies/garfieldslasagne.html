Garfield's Lasagne

The iconic lasagne is very filling as well as ABSOULTELY MASSIVE

Ingredients:

  • Sheet-Style Pasta
  • Cream Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Tupperware

Steps:

  1. Place a sheet of pasta on the bottom of the tupperware
  2. Layer with both pasta sauce and cream cheese
  3. Contiously do and cap off with a sheet of pasta on top
  4. Drizzle olive oil and spread cheese on top
  5. Bake in over at 465 degrees for 1 hour
